Smart Travel Tips

Digital Tools

Use festival apps for tickets, maps, and real-time updates. Download before travel for offline access.

Safety First

Follow health guidelines and stay updated on local protocols. Keep emergency contacts saved in your phone.

Learn about local customs and traditions before visiting. Dress appropriately for religious festivals.
